Any concerning behavior?

Adoption
Has Outcome to Adoption been started?
You should be on the Outcome to Adoption screen for the cat(s).
  • Make sure the correct cat(s) are selected.
  • Confirm the adopter’s Person record is linked to this Outcome.
Adoption
Have vouchers been created for each cat?
Each cat should have the correct voucher(s) attached.
  • Check the Fees section for each cat.
  • Verify fee amounts match the intended adoption fee.
  • Confirm any discounted or special pricing is applied correctly.
Adoption
Is microchip consent and Petco choice completed?
Confirm microchip and 24PetWatch information is completed accurately.
  • Confirm the adopter understands microchip registration.
  • Confirm Petco choice has been selected if applicable.
Adoption
Is the AV/Petco assistance form prepared?
Make sure the AV (Adoption Voucher) and Petco assistance information are ready.
  • Confirm AV form is printed or visible for review.
  • Make sure support information is ready to show the adopter.
Adoption
Has the adoption contract been reviewed and signed?
Have the adopter read the legally required contract language and sign it. Review what is being sent to the adopter: • Medical History Report • Your Adoption Contract • 24 PetWatch registration information • If Avimark record is emailed, check that "E-mail Sent" is there before closing the page.
Adoption
Is the payment slip (paper) completed and payment processed?
Payment should be collected and recorded correctly:
  • Confirm the amount matches the adoption fee and any additional services.
  • Record payment method (card/cash/other) as required.
  • Make sure the paper slip is filled out completely.
Sending kitty home
Is the cat ready to go home?
Make sure the cat is safely prepared to leave with the adopter.
  • Ensure the cat is safely in a carrier.
  • Confirm the adopter has all belongings (toys, bed, etc. if provided).
After adopter leaves
Has the adopter memo been completed?
Summarize key details about the adopter in the memo.
  • Note relevant details that will help staff in the future (housing, lifestyle, expectations).
After adopter leaves
Has the cat memo been updated?
Record important cat-specific notes.
  • Include behavior notes, medical updates, or special care instructions.

  • Confirm association in PetPoint.

Make sure the ADA is scanned or uploaded correctly.
  • Confirm the ADA is legible and correctly attached to the record.
After adopter leaves

  • Confirm your scanned or uploaded consultation form is readable.
After adopter leaves
Has the receipt been attached or uploaded?
Confirm the payment receipt is saved in the correct place.
  • Attach receipt to the adopter’s record.
  • Verify receipt details match the adoption fee and services.
After adopter leaves
Has the calendar been updated?
Update the calendar so all staff see the adoption.
  • Confirm the correct cat(s) and adopter’s name are on the calendar entry.
After adopter leaves
Has the photo been texted to Kim?
Send a quick photo of the adopter and cat, if taken.
  • Confirm the text has been sent successfully.
After adopter leaves
Has the Outlook follow up email been sent?
Send the standard follow up email to the adopter.
  • Confirm the adopter’s email address is correct.
  • Confirm the correct template was used.
After adopter leaves
Have the payment & slips been filed?
Make sure all physical paperwork and payment slips are stored correctly.
  • Place payment slips in the correct folder or box.
  • Confirm any related forms are in the right day’s batch.
After adopter leaves
Have whiteboards been updated?
Update any room or lobby boards that track cats and adoptions.
  • Remove adopted cats from boards where they appear.
  • Add any new notes or messages as needed.
No adoption
Wrap up – no adoption
Use this screen when the adopter does not take a cat home today.
  • Thank the adopter for visiting.
  • Encourage them to check the website or visit again.
  • Offer any additional information that might help them in the future.
Pause
Stop and notify staff
Use this screen when there is a concern about the adopter or situation.
  • Pause the meet and greet as needed.
  • Notify a senior staff member right away.
  • Follow staff instructions before resuming or ending the interaction.
